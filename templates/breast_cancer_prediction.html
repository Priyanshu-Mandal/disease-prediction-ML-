BREAST CANCER DISEASE PREDICTION



1."Radius_Mean" is the average distance from the center of the nucleus to the perimeter of cancer cell clusters.


2."Perimeter_Mean" refers to the average length of the boundary of cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


3."Area_Mean" refers to the average size of the area occupied by cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


4."Compactness_Mean" refers to the ratio of the perimeter squared to the area of cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


5."Concavity_Mean" refers to the average severity of concave portions of the cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


6."Concave_points_Mean" refers to the average number of concave portions of the cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


7."Radius_se" refers to the standard error of distances from the center to points on the perimeter of cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


8."Perimeter_se" refers to the standard error of the length of the boundary of cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


9."Area_se" refers to the standard error of the size of the area occupied by cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


10."Radius_Worst" refers to the largest distance from the center of the nucleus to the perimeter of cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


11."Perimeter_Worst" refers to the largest length of the boundary of cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


12."Area_Worst" refers to the largest size of the area occupied by cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


13."Compactness_Worst" refers to the ratio of the perimeter squared to the area of the largest cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


14."Concavity_Worst" refers to the largest severity of concave portions of the cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.


15."Concave_points_Worst" refers to the largest number of concave portions of the cancer cell clusters in a sample of breast tissue.
