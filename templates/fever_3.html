FEVER MEDICATION INFORMATION



AGE (Adults)

Age group of 17+ Years


What To Do

Age group : 17+ years

Temperature : Up to 102 F (38.9 C) Taken Orally

1. Rest and drink plenty of fluids.
2. Medication isn't needed.
3. Call the doctor if the fever is accompanied by a severe headache, stiff neck, shortness of breath, or other unusual signs or symptoms.


Temperature : Above 102 F (38.9 C) Taken Orally

1. If you're uncomfortable, take acetaminophen (Tylenol, others), ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin IB, others) or aspirin.
2. Read the label carefully for proper dosage, and be careful not to take more than one medication containing acetaminophen, such as some cough and cold medicines.
3. Call the doctor if the fever doesn't respond to the medication, is consistently 103 F (39.4 C) or higher, or lasts longer than three days.
